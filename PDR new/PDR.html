Description

Doing an entire group project online without ever having met before in real life is something new. Having to keep your presence aparent in meetings and assuring participation was a challenge.
over time you find more efficient working ways. Mainly having live documents that people can work in simultanious. such as Canva.com, by simply sharing the link to the file youre working om, everyone can join in and work on it at the same time. Also google docs for making reports at the same time. while together in a teams meeting and working together in the same file. gives a real "working together"  feel. Not every medium form of media has a online working together system. somethings just need to be done in person, such as discussing certain choices for colors and design and stuff. meeting and campus was therefor an option. Even though not everyone was available every single time. it is better to actually communicate with everyone at the same time. So doing the real life meetings with who can and online for those who cant and try to make the best of things. 
For the prototyping we were able to work in adobe xd together in an online sharing folder. while being in a microsoft teams meeting.

After getting to know the team more over time. Working becomes allot easier. Being able to voice opinions better and dividing workforce. Also finding common interests outside of the projects helps for a more friendly look upon things.

For the next time I work in group ill be more involved from the start. instead of letting it slowly grow. Working in a nice group format from the beginning in better than starting late.
